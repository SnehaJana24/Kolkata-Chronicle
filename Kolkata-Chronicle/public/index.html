About Kolkata Chronicle

Kolkata Chronicle is an immersive heritage platform that allows you to travel through time and visually experience how Kolkata evolved from 1900 to 1990. It blends history with technology in a fun and interactive way.

With our split-view engine and time slider, you can compare Old Kolkata vs Modern Kolkata instantly. The platform also features ambient soundscapes, historical stories, and fully curated timelines of iconic locations.

  • View historic reconstruction of 20+ famous places
  • Hear tram, river, temple and street soundscapes
  • Read time-based identity, uniqueness & narratives
  • Explore with map navigation and auto-time-travel buttons
